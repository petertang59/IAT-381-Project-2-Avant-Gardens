Hi there! We're just a small, local flower store located in Burnaby. We pride ourselves on our many available arrangements that are sure to please for any occasion!

We started right from the very back-garden of our family house, when one fateful day someone requested a small bouquet of roses, and things just sprouted from there! The store is run by the Smith Family: Bruce, Alfred, Dick and Barbara. Each of whom is just as passionate as the other in regards to arranging flowers.

So take your time and enjoy perusing the many beautiful arrangements we have available for all your flowery needs!

Bruce Smith

Flower arranger and Manager

Bruce is an avid enthusiast in the field of flower arrangement. With a back- ground in biology and design, his knowledge in the area has proven time and time again to yield some of the most creative arrangements seen on the internet.

He also has his own YouTube Channel with a series of tutorials on how to arrange flowers in a professional manner.

Alfred Smith

Flower arranger and Caretaker

Alfred is also an expert in the field of flower arrangement, and has a ever-burning passion for floral aesthetics. Like his brother, he also has a background in biology and design and has ensured that both these areas of knowledge are applied in his work.

He is also a part-time gardener and has worked for all sorts of clients, from small backyards to school fields.
